This is still a work in progress. Since the boom in LLMs I have somehow had more confidence in my options and abilities to build things.

JournalClub therefore uses an LLM to summarise any journal article that is either uploaded or imported from pubmed using a DOI or a PMID. It is meant to save clinicians the trouble of trawling through articles instead offering people the option to listen to articles either summarised for them or read to them.

This is a FastAPI server.
